• Select Email Id mapped to HealthIndia location / nearest HealthIndia location from the Information sheet.
• Kindly mention the following information in the subject line

Claim TypeSubject Line FormatExample
For New / Fresh claim submissionOnline Submission | <mention your Tpa Member Id> | <Date of Admission> | FRESHOnline Submission | 123456EM | 07/04/2020 | FRESH
For Deficient / Query document claim submissionOnline Submission | <mention your Tpa Member Id> | <Date of Admission> | DEFICIENTOnline Submission | 123456EM | 07/04/2020 | DEFICIENT

• Please scan your documents in below sequence:
Claim Form | Discharge Summary | Final Bill | Investigation Reports | Doctor Consultation Papers | Sticker/Invoice- For Implant | Others



How to create .pdf file

• If you have accessibility to scanner, scan your document as single pdf file (for document clarity scan the document at 200 dpi)
• Scanning can be done using your mobile phone. Download Cam Scanner from play store or apple store on your mobile. For merging / creating multiple page single pdf file, you need to use the feature collage which can be brought at a small price for a period of 7 day trial.
• Cam Scanner -

  1. 1. Set the Docs scan feature
  2. 2. Focus on the document, click and Save
  3. 3. Select Continue and complete
  4. 4. Select collage and click on the scan pages to create a single pdf document
